Established in 2020, Hidden Gems Safaris is a leading full-service domestic tour operator offering a comprehensive range of high quality tour products throughout Kenya.

The company specializes in designing unique tailor-made tour packages for guided safaris to fit every type of tourist with affordable prices to have splendid safaris ranging from the wild to the beach.

As one of the most trusted domestic tour operator in Kenya we offer our clients exceptional service; reliability; expert knowledge, both of the destinations in which we operate, and of the management of tour operations in these regions; as well as value for money.

Our expert consultants have knowledge of the destinations and our clients’ unique requirements, work quickly and accurately, and take responsibility to ensure that all services are thoroughly checked.
